To run this project:

1. Open https://helenag0.github.io/Si379/finalproject/ in your browser
2. Navigate through the maze using WASD or your arrow keys
3. Your cursor is a little white circle in the center of your screen. To complete the maze, line the cursor up with the red pillar and click once
4. You will see confetti and a 'restart maze?' text. Line the cursor up with the text and click to reset the maze.
